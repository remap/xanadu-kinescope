Section Moves Inspiration
Wind Dance
  • Windmills : Arms in a clockwise motion, stepping side to side.
  • Tiptoe : In place, raise one arm high, sweep it down and sideways.
The winds move in dances, a honey-tongued voice arises...
March & Rock
  • Marching : In place, elbows bent, hands mimic spear-light beams.
  • Rock : Forward and back, fists raised triumphantly overhead.
Light of spears through rocks shadows, endless light of hope victory.
Circle & Reach
  • Step-Touch : Side to side, arms making gentle forward circles.
  • Reach & Pull : Alternating, as if grasping distant tales.
Echo of ancient times, tales of the past wither.
Tap & Pivot
  • Tap : Left foot forward and back, raising arms open wide.
  • Pivot : Surveying, one hand to heart, then extend outward.
The fame of a foreseeing man worthy work in great words, passion and time in hope.
The winds move in dances,
Οἱ ἄνεμοι ἐν χοροῖς κινοῦνται,
a honey-tongued voice arises from there,
φωνὴ μελίγλωσσος ἔνθεν αἴρει,
in the shade, tales of the past wither.
ἐν σκιᾷ φθιμένας ἱστορίαι.

Light of spears through rocks shadows,
Φῶς δοράτων σκιάζει, διὰ βράχους,
echo of ancient times,
ἡχώ τῶν καιρῶν παλαιῶν,
endless light of hope victory.
ἐλπίδων ἀέναον φῶς νίκης.

The fame of a foreseeing man
Ἀνδρὸς προμηθῆος φήμη
worthy work in great words,
ἄξιον ἔργον ἐν λόγῳ μέγα,
passion and time in hope.
πάθος καὶ χρόνος ἐν ἐλπίδι.
