Wave Flow
Wave Reach:
  • Wide stance, arms extended, sway side to side with waves.
Melody Step:
  • Step forward and back, lift arms upward and down with tune.
In a wave of light, you see a man sent. A blessed voice, the sweet melody.
Color Echo
Hues Spin:
  • Spin slowly, arms extended, imagine bright colors around.
Memory Sway:
  • Sway side to side, creating memory threads with hands.
A chorus sings, clothed in bright hues. Honey of the throat to every memory.
Golden Glide
Golden Shadow Stroll:
  • Stroll forward, cast an imaginary shadow with raised arms.
Mirror Balance:
  • Balance on one leg, reflect the sky, alternate gracefully.
A small god, a bright shadow in gold. Mirror of the beautiful sky does govern.
Weaving Joy
Weave Step:
  • Step side to side, trace weaving patterns with hands.
Playful Spin:
  • Spin playfully, incorporate a light jump.
Weaves conflict and play together.

Σε κύμα φωτός ἄνδρα στῆλεν ὁρᾷς,

In a wave of light, you see a man sent,

ὅλβιον φωνήν, τὸ μέλος ἡδέως.

A blessed voice, the sweet melody.

Κύκλῳ πέπλων λείπει ψυχῆς εὐφροσύνην.

In the circle of garments, leaves the joy of the soul.

Χορὸς ἀείδει, χρόας λαμπρῶν ἐνδεδυμένος,

A chorus sings, clothed in bright hues,

Μέλι τοῦ λαιμοῦ ποτὶ πάσης μνήμης,

Honey of the throat to every memory,

Ἰλύς χρονίων ὑμνείᾳ πάλιν ἄγει.

Clay of ages brings forth a hymn anew.

Μικρὸς θεός, λαμπρὰ σκιὰ ἐν χρυσῷ,

A small god, a bright shadow in gold,

Καθρέπτης τοῦ αἰθέρος καλοῦ ἄρχει,

Mirror of the beautiful sky does govern,

Νεῖκος καὶ παιδιάν συνυφαίνει.

Weaves conflict and play together.
