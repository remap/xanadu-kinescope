Light Sway

  • Slow step-touches while bringing arms down, crossing over the heart and opening wide.

Dawn Reach
  • Twisting side-to-side with quick steps, reaching forward as if grasping dawn's light.
  • Smooth forward strides , speaking with hands that cross over the chest and extend outward.
"Πρὸς ὄρθριον φέγγος ὁρᾷς,"
"Δρόμῳ λέγουσα λόγους γλυκερούς,"
Wind Flow
  • Gentle arm circles to mimic the flow of air, in sync with steps lightly bouncing side-to-side.
  • Hands sway like whispers , pivoting in place to emulate the unfolding of winds.

"Ἀνέμων ψίθυροι πέπλωνται,"
Final Flourish
  • Rhythmic clapping above the head, followed by a quick heel-tap motion.
  • Wide steps and jazz hands reaching outwards to embrace the light of music.
"Χεῖρες κινήσεις ῥυθμῷ,"
"Μουσικῆς κάλλιστον φῶς."

In the midst of white light you stand,
Ἐν μέσῳ φωτὶ λευκῷ στάσεις,
A song of melancholy singing,
Φωνὴ μέλεος ἀείδων,
And the heart beats.
Καὶ πάλλεται καρδία.

Towards dawn's light you gaze,
Πρὸς ὄρθριον φέγγος ὁρᾷς,
Uttering sweet words swiftly,
Δρόμῳ λέγουσα λόγους γλυκερούς,
Air, a linen of the wind.
Ἀήρ σινδὼν ἀνεμόεν.

Hands move in rhythm,
Χεῖρες κινήσεις ῥυθμῷ,
And whispers of winds unfold,
Καὶ ἀνέμων ψίθυροι πέπλωνται,
Music's most beautiful light.
Μουσικῆς κάλλιστον φῶς.
